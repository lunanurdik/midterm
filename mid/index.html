About Bookcrossing

We are the ones who protect the written culture.

The exchange of books is now not very popular among the general population. We want to provide this opportunity through our service. Why do you think you need to exchange books? This brings only one positive results: environmental friendliness, financial savings, a new way of finding people according to book priorities.

You can fill out a questionnaire when exchanging books, so that the next reader can contact you and discuss, or maybe even become a friend.
